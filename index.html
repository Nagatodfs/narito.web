History The University of Eastern Pangasinan is the first institution of higher education in the town of Binalonan and the second one in Pangasinan to adopt a public-private partnership wherein the government assumes substantial financial grants and private entities take on the operations of the university. The establishment of UEP began in 2005 following a dissertation paper authored by its founding president, Dr. Ramon V. Guico III, structured around the idea of creating a world-class institution that will provide the best practices in education to students regardless of their social, cultural and financial background.
